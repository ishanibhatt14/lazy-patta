# Marketing & App Store Optimization

Grow through **warmth and word-of-mouth within families**, not aggressive
performance marketing. The invite loop _is_ the growth engine.

## Positioning

- **Promise:** "The Indian card games families grew up playing, made easy to play
  together from anywhere."
- **Differentiator:** Gujarati-first personality, family warmth, and a hard
  **no-casino** stance — the opposite of Teen Patti/rummy clones.
- **Tagline:** _Ghar ni ramato. Badha sathe._ · support line: _The card games we
  grew up playing._

## Growth engine: the family invite loop

The strongest channel is a relative sharing a **room link on WhatsApp**. Optimize it
relentlessly:

- One-tap **share** of room code/link; works cold (fresh install, not signed in) via
  `/invite/[code]` deep links ([IA](../03-ux-specification/information-architecture.md)).
- Warm, low-friction join → auth → lobby; intent preserved through sign-in.
- Delightful, screenshot-worthy moments (the **Gadha Chor** reveal, pair confetti)
  that families want to share.

## App Store Optimization (ASO)

- **Icon:** mascot-forward, warm cream/maroon — instantly distinct from casino
  red-and-gold on the shelf ([brand](../01-brand/brand-guidelines.md)).
- **Screenshots:** real gameplay + the warm reveal + "play with family, anywhere" +
  the safety line "No cash. No betting. Just family fun." Show EN/GU/HI.
- **Keywords:** desi/Indian/Gujarati family card games, Gadha Chor, Gulam Chor, play
  with family — **avoid** gambling/casino/real-money terms (both off-brand and a
  review risk).
- **Localized listings:** EN/GU/HI store metadata, localized separately from in-app
  copy ([voice](../01-brand/voice-and-copywriting.md)).
- **Accurate age rating + data-safety** answers; **clear "no cash, no betting"**
  positioning ([security-and-privacy](../05-architecture/security-and-privacy.md)).
- **Reviewer demo account/mode** so review passes smoothly.

## Channels (in priority order)

1. **In-family virality** — invites, rematches, "teach the kids" moments.
2. **Diaspora communities** — Gujarati/Indian community groups, festival timing
   (Diwali, Uttarayan) tied to festival themes.
3. **Organic content** — short clips of the warm reveal; the mascot as a shareable
   character.
4. **Cross-promotion** — gentle links from **Lazy Traveler** and future Lazy apps
   ([ecosystem](../README.md#the-lazy-ecosystem)).

## Seasonal moments

Festival windows are peak family-together time. Align releases/themes/pushes with
**Diwali** first, then **Uttarayan/Makar Sankranti**, **Holi**, **Navratri** — each
ties to a [festival theme](../02-design-system/themes.md) and an achievement.

## Brand-safe marketing rules

- Never imply gambling, winnings, or money.
- No FOMO/urgency dark patterns; notifications are opt-in and non-marketing in MVP
  ([screen catalog · notifications](../03-ux-specification/screen-catalog.md)).
- Every asset passes the [brand checklist](../01-brand/brand-guidelines.md#brand-checklist-paste-into-any-review).
