# Monetization

**Constraint first:** no cash, betting, wagering, prize pools, coins, loot boxes, or
casino mechanics — ever ([non-negotiables](../00-product-bible/01-vision-and-mission.md#what-we-are-not--non-negotiables)).
No ads in the first release. Monetization must never compromise the "grandparents'
house" feeling or the family-safety promise.

## Philosophy

Charge for **delight and belonging**, never for **advantage or relief from
friction we manufactured**. Nothing paid may create pay-to-win, gate the core family
experience, or introduce dark patterns. A grandparent must be able to play fully,
free, forever.

## MVP: free, no ads

- The full Gadha Chor experience (guest vs computer + private live rooms) is **free**.
- Goal in Q1 is **love and retention**, not revenue. Monetization is deliberately
  deferred so first impressions are pure.

## Post-MVP candidate models (evaluate, don't assume)

Ranked by fit with brand values:

1. **Cosmetic & seasonal (best fit).** Optional premium **card backs**, **table
   themes**, **festival packs**, **mascot outfits**, **avatar sets**. Purely visual,
   zero gameplay effect. Aligns with the design-system theming pipeline
   ([themes](../02-design-system/themes.md)).
2. **"Lazy Patta Family" subscription.** A gentle supporter tier: all cosmetics,
   early access to new games, the recorded **Gujarati voice pack**, extra family-group
   slots. Everything gameplay-essential stays free.
3. **Voice/content packs.** One-time purchases (e.g. recorded voice pack, extra
   tutorials/coach). Delight, not advantage.
4. **New games** may be free or one-time unlocks — decided per game; never a wall in
   front of the family core.

## Explicitly rejected

- ❌ Real-money gaming / betting / prizes (illegal-adjacent + off-brand).
- ❌ Coins/chips/energy/loot boxes or any RNG purchase.
- ❌ Pay-to-win (better bots, better odds, "skip the donkey").
- ❌ Ads in the family game surface (especially anything gambling-adjacent).
- ❌ Manipulative streaks, FOMO timers, or nag screens.

## Guardrails for any paid feature

- **Cosmetic-only** or **content-only**; never advantage.
- **Core family play stays free** and complete.
- **Kid-safe purchasing:** respect platform parental controls; no manipulative
  prompts; clear, honest pricing.
- **Store-compliant** data-safety and age settings ([security](../05-architecture/security-and-privacy.md)).

## Success = love before money

Monetization is gated behind **retention proof** (see
[analytics](./analytics-and-kpis.md)). If families aren't coming back for free, no
store fixes that — so Q1 optimizes for the emotional and retention signals, and any
revenue experiment in Q2+ must not move those the wrong way.
