# Monetization Readiness

This branch does not add checkout, subscriptions, advertisements, or prices. The
core game remains fair and non-pay-to-win.

Future extension points can be layered around, not inside, game authority:

- Family Pass entitlement for convenience and hosting benefits
- Premium themes
- Host-paid room benefits that do not affect game outcomes
- Ad-free entitlement
- Cosmetic card backs
- Festival table themes
- House-rule presets

Do not add Stripe, RevenueCat, Google Play Billing, App Store billing,
advertising SDKs, placeholder purchase buttons, or pay-to-win mechanics without
a separate product and compliance review.
