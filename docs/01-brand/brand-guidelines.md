# Brand Guidelines

## Brand in one line

**Modern Indian folk, warm and nostalgic** — a Gujarati family game night you can
open from anywhere.

## Brand personality

If Lazy Patta were a person, they'd be the fun aunt/uncle (_kaka/kaki_, _mama/masi_)
who keeps the whole family playing late into the night — warm, a little cheeky,
never mean, and quietly excellent at making everyone feel included.

| Trait  | We are…                                   | We are not…                  |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Warmth | homely, affectionate, inviting            | cold, corporate, clinical    |
| Humor  | gentle, playful, self-aware (the donkey!) | mocking, edgy, crude         |
| Craft  | premium, considered, handmade-feeling     | cheap, cluttered, templated  |
| Energy | cozy and unhurried                        | frantic, hype, "win big"     |
| Tone   | plainspoken, family-first                 | jargon-y, salesy, aggressive |

### The five brand values, applied

- **Simple** — a grandparent gets it without help.
- **Warm** — folk craft and family language over spectacle.
- **Family** — designed for many people, many ages, one table.
- **Nostalgic** — it should trigger a memory, not just a session.
- **Premium** — restraint, spacing, and polish signal quality without gold.

## The non-casino line (hard rules)

This is the single most important brand boundary. See
[decisions-log D-02](../00-product-bible/decisions-log.md).

**Never use:**

- Poker chips, gold coins, or any currency imagery.
- Slot-machine / jackpot / spinning-reel motifs.
- Casino neon, glossy gold gradients, felt-and-velvet "high roller" styling.
- "Win big," "jackpot," "bet," "stakes," "double down," or similar language.
- Aggressive reds/blacks as the dominant palette.

**Instead use** (art direction detail in
[illustration-and-texture](./illustration-and-texture.md)):

- **Warm cream paper** backgrounds and soft, shallow shadows.
- **Bandhani-inspired dots** and simple **geometric folk borders**.
- **Ajrakh-style block-print** motifs as subtle texture/accents.
- Festival warmth: string lights (_toran_), diyas — tasteful, never gaudy.
- The **donkey mascot** for humor instead of flashy reward animations.
- Craft references: warm wood, handloom fabric, chai, khakhra — _home_, not venue.

## Visual identity summary

The precise values are tokens; here is the intent. Exact hex/spacing/type live in
[design-tokens](../02-design-system/design-tokens.md).

- **Signature colors:** cream canvas, deep maroon (primary), saffron (accent, dark
  text on it), teal (secondary action), felt green (the table). Saffron is
  decorative or paired with dark text — never small saffron text on cream.
- **Type:** a warm rounded display face for headings; a highly legible sans for UI.
  Full Gujarati (Noto Sans Gujarati) and Devanagari (Noto Sans Devanagari) coverage
  is mandatory, not optional.
- **Shape language:** rounded corners everywhere (12 controls / 20 cards & sheets /
  28 hero panels), generous spacing on an 8-pt grid, soft shadows.
- **Texture:** cream paper grain, subtle Bandhani dot fields, folk borders — always
  in service of legibility, never competing with content.

## Logo & lockups

_Asset production pending brand sign-off (decisions D-01/D-04). Rules to hold to:_

- **Wordmark:** "Lazy Patta" in the display face; optional descriptor "Desi Card
  Games" in the UI sans beneath.
- **Mascot lockup:** Gaddo the donkey peeking beside/behind a fan of cards.
- **Clear space:** minimum = the height of the "L" on all sides.
- **Minimum sizes:** wordmark ≥ 96px wide on screen; app icon must remain legible at 48px.
- **Don't:** stretch, recolor outside the palette, add drop shadows/outer glows, or
  place the wordmark on low-contrast/ busy backgrounds.
- **App icon:** mascot-forward, warm, instantly distinct from casino red-and-gold —
  our shelf differentiator (see [07 · ASO](../07-product-strategy/marketing-and-aso.md)).

## Naming & terminology

- Product: **Lazy Patta** (descriptor _Desi Card Games_).
- Franchise: **Lazy** ecosystem (see [ecosystem](../README.md#the-lazy-ecosystem)).
- The loser of a Gadha Chor round is the **Gadha Chor** — always framed with
  affection and humor, never shame. Gentle ribbing, family-style.
- Prefer the game's own vocabulary (_jodi_ = pair, _varo_ = turn) alongside English.

## Brand checklist (paste into any review)

- [ ] Would this feel at home at a family Diwali table?
- [ ] Zero casino/currency imagery or language?
- [ ] Warm palette, soft shadows, folk texture in service of legibility?
- [ ] Humor is gentle and inclusive (never mocking the loser cruelly)?
- [ ] Legible and usable for a grandparent and a child?
- [ ] Colors/type/spacing all drawn from tokens, not one-offs?
