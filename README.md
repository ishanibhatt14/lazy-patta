# Lazy Patta

> **Ghar ni ramato. Badha sathe.** — _The card games we grew up playing._

A premium, nostalgic multiplayer platform for the traditional Indian family card
games we grew up playing — starting with **Gadha Chor**. Play against the computer
or in private rooms with family, anywhere.

**Lazy Patta is not a gambling app.** No cash, no betting, no coins, no casino. The
feeling we're building for: _playing cards at your grandparents' house during Diwali._

Part of the **Lazy** ecosystem (alongside [Lazy Traveler](https://lazytraveler.app)).
